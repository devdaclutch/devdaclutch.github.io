About Me

Hi there! I'm a third-year Computer Engineering student at the University of California, Santa Cruz, with a minor in Computer Science. My coding journey began in high school, where I founded a coding club and developed programs to inspire others to explore technology. Since then, I've gained experience in Python, C, JavaScript, HTML, and various other technologies. My passion for Data Science was sparked by a boot camp that built a strong foundation in Python and data analysis. In addition to my academic pursuits, I have hands-on experience editing YouTube videos for a well-known creator, which honed my communication and self-accountability skills. When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing basketball, gaming, and creating content. My ultimate vision is to merge my technical expertise with my hobbies to create innovative and impactful solutions.
